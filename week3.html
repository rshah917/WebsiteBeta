Fortnite Weekly Challenges

     Week 3

      Free Pass

Use high damage dealing weapons for this challange, such AR's, Shotguns, RPG's, Gernade Launchers, etc.

Launchpads are best found in 50 v 50. The inner final circle contains numerous supply drops, from which launch pads can be easily acquired. In a regular game mode, supply drops, llamas, and vending machines are the easiest way to do this challange.

      Battle Pass

Most chests are located inside of buildings, in the surrounding forests, and in side of small sheds/barns.

Various clay pigeons are located throughout the map. Activate the machine and fire at the disk like object it fires out.

Try to land first in order to pick up a weapon before your opponents and shoot them down before they can get one.

Only RPG's or Gernade Launchers may be used in order to complete this challenge.
