Fortnite Weekly Challenges

     Week 4

      Free Pass

For this challange you will need 2,500 materials. This challange will be easily completed over 3-4 games withouut much effort, as long as you build in fights.

Using an ATK or Shopping Cart, you must drive through the various fire hoops on the map. If using a shopping cart, it is easier to build up into the hoop, as the provided ramps usually only work with ATK's.

Try to land first in order to pick up a weapon before your opponents and shoot them down before they can get one.

      Battle Pass

When using a sniper rifle, dealing headshots do insane amounts of damage.

Silenced pistols deal the most damage, along with dual pistols.
